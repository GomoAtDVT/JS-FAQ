Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the frequently asked questions:

What is the capital of South Africa?
The capital of South Africa is Pretoria and the largest city is Johannesburg. with three capital cities: Pretoria, Bloemfontein, and Cape Town.
What is the capital of the United States?

The capital of the United States is Washington D.C.

What is the capital of the United Kingdom?

The capital of the United Kingdom is London. Known for its history, culture, and architecture.

What is the capital of France?

The capital of France is Paris.

What is the capital of Germany?

The capital of Germany is Berlin with the largest city being Berlin. Finally, being known for its rich history and culture.

What is the capital of Italy?

The capital of Italy is Rome.
